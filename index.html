Luka Modrić

Croatian Midfield Maestro

Biography

Luka Modrić was born on September 9, 1985, in Zadar, Croatia. Known for his vision, passing, and leadership, he began his professional career at Dinamo Zagreb before moving to Tottenham Hotspur and then Real Madrid in 2012. He is widely considered one of the best midfielders in football history.
